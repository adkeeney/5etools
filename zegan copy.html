    Otrix Emberwoods

    Description

    Otrix stands 6 ft tall and has a medium build. He has long brown hair and brown eyes. He appears to be in his late 20's. He wears scale armor and occasionally a shield, both have the insignia of the Royal House of Draford. He carries an immaculate maul that he is very proficient with. He wears the amulet of the Draford Royal House around his neck.

    Otrix looks intimidating and powerful but in reality he is calm and docile. He hates confrontation and prefers to talk himself out of situations. He can be very indecisive and his mind can be changed easily with rational suggestions. Otrix is a quiet and private person and prefers seclusion to read or write books.

    Background

    Otrix's parents and grandparents are descendants of the original settlers of Draford. Otrix grew up in a rich household with proper training in language, Cleric skills, fighting, reading and writing. He was groomed by his father to take over as one of the lead advisors to the Draford Royal House. Otrix took this position upon reaching maturity and has been faithful to the Draford Royal Family helping advise on strategic decisions.

    Recently, Otrix has started questioning his role in Draford and he is disgusted with the neverending war that Draford is fighting against the Gunula Family over control of the Grimstone Mine. Otrix still advises the Queen and the Royal Family but otherwise secludes himself in his room scribbling in his notebook and staring out his window overlooking Draford harbor.
